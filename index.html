About Wangari & Paws & Hooves

Founded with a passion for animal welfare, Paws & Hooves has been serving the Kenyan community for over 5 years. Dr. Wangari established this clinic to bridge the gap between premium veterinary care and accessibility.

Our Mission

To provide compassionate, professional, and accessible veterinary care to all animals while building strong relationships with pet owners and farmers in our community.

Why Choose Us?

  • ✓ Licensed Veterinarians: Professional and experienced team
  • ✓ Modern Equipment: Advanced diagnostic and surgical tools
  • ✓ Affordable Care: Competitive pricing without compromising quality
  • ✓ 24/7 Emergency: Always available when you need us
  • ✓ Farm Visits: On-site livestock consultations
  • ✓ Compassionate Approach: Stress-free care for animals
